Rod Wave

Rod Wave, born Rodarius Marcell Green, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter known for his deeply emotional lyrics and melodic style. Emerging from St. Petersburg, Florida, he gained widespread recognition with his 2019 hit **"Heart on Ice,"** which went viral on social media and charted on the Billboard Hot 100. His music blends rap and R&B, often featuring introspective storytelling about personal struggles, success, and resilience. Wave's soulful delivery and heartfelt lyrics have earned him a dedicated fanbase, with albums like *SoulFly* and *Beautiful Mind* topping the charts. His unique sound, often characterized by rich instrumentation and powerful vocal performances, sets him apart in the hip-hop industry.
